Business Analyst

Overview
  • This role involves working with the SLS corporate team to develop business cases, manage potential and existing customers. The successful candidate will be performing research as directed for new projects or business opportunities, be involved in project management and will also be assigned cross-functional duties including software testing and documentation.
Responsibilities
  • Contacting potential/existing clients to establish rapport and set up meetings.
  • Recording notes and minutes of meetings both internal and external.
  • Prepare for and attend presentations for sales and project activities.
  • Attend conferences, meetings and industry events for information gathering and research.
  • Responsibile for project tasks and ensuring they are completed in a timely fashion.
  • Track overall project timelines, create and maintain executive dashboards and followup with stakeholders to ensure the project remains on schedule. Work with the management team to formulate recommended actions and risk mitigation in cases where milestones will be missed.
  • Monitor project spending and track against its assigned budget.
  • Assist the QA (Quality Assurance) team in the execution and documentation of software tests. Identify and assist in troubleshooting of any issues that are identified.
Requirements
  • Diploma or degree in Finance, Business, Information Systems, Computer Science, Engineering or equivalent
  • Superior command of English language both written and verbal
  • Strong communication skills
  • IT Fluency and Cloud Computing knowledge is an asset
  • Attention to detail
  • Excels in time management and demonstrated capability to prioritize tasks to meet deadlines
  • Strong analytical thinking and problem solving skills
  • Able to work in a dynamic, fast moving and challenging environment with minimal supervision
